I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Hong Kong Polytechnic University. I am deeply passionate about understanding the intricate mechanisms underlying neural disorders and developing innovative solutions that empower individuals to live more fulfilling lives. My work is driven by a strong belief in the transformative potential of interdisciplinary research at the intersection of neural engineering, rehabilitation science, robotics, artificial intelligence, wearable technology, neuroscience, and neuroimaging.

Research Focus
======
- **Stroke Rehabilitation**: Sensorimotor-integrated approaches for upper limb recovery
- **Wearable Robotics**: Exoskeletons and assistive devices for rehabilitation
- **Biosignal Processing**: EMG, EEG, and corticomuscular coherence analysis
- **Neuromodulation**: Focal vibratory and electrical stimulation for sensory rehabilitation
